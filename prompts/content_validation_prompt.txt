Validate the optimized content for accuracy, brand compliance, and AI Overview readiness.

ORIGINAL_CONTENT: {original_content}
OPTIMIZED_CONTENT: {optimized_content}
TARGET_QUERY: {target_query}
SUB_INTENT: {sub_intent}
OPTIMIZATION_GOALS: {optimization_goals}

VALIDATION CRITERIA:
1. Factual accuracy and information correctness
2. ING brand voice and messaging consistency
3. SEO optimization quality and keyword integration
4. AI Overview inclusion readiness (structure, format, completeness)
5. User intent fulfillment and value delivery
6. Content quality and readability
7. Competitive positioning effectiveness
8. Technical SEO elements (headings, structure, etc.)

SPECIFIC VALIDATION CHECKS:
- Does content accurately answer the target query?
- Is ING brand positioning clear and compelling?
- Are optimization recommendations properly implemented?
- Is content structure optimal for AI Overview extraction?
- Are there any factual errors or inconsistencies?
- Does content provide clear value to users?
- Is the content comprehensive enough for the target intent?
- Are technical SEO elements properly implemented?

Return ONLY valid JSON:
